Our New Dental Technologies

At 100SMILES Dental Care, we are committed to staying at the forefront of dental innovation. These modern technologies help us diagnose accurately, treat comfortably, and deliver the highest standard of care to every patient.

Digital X-Rays

High-resolution imaging with significantly less radiation compared to traditional X-rays. Enables faster and more accurate diagnosis for early intervention and effective treatment.

Intraoral Cameras

Detailed images of teeth and gums help us detect early problems and explain treatment options clearly, improving patient understanding and comfort.

Laser Dentistry

Minimally invasive procedures with reduced pain, less bleeding, and quicker healing. Ideal for gum treatments, cavity detection, and whitening.

CAD/CAM Technology

Custom crowns, veneers, and restorations designed and manufactured in a single visit. Precision, comfort, and a natural look — all in one appointment.

3D Printing

Used to create accurate dental models, surgical guides, and appliances. Enables precise planning and improved treatment outcomes.

Teledentistry

Virtual consultations that allow patients to access dental care from home. Ideal for quick advice, treatment planning, and follow-up assessments.

Want to find out which technology suits your dental needs? Contact us or book an appointment — our team is ready to help.
